- ongoing work -

SWOOPE, “ This work challenges my life-long relationship with basketball and my physical inability to dunk. Taking a heuristic and dramatic approach towards my dream of slam dunking, I fabricate bio mechanical apparatuses that augment my forces while following my parameters; rule #1 must leave ground from the bottom of sneaker, rule #2 must be an official 10’ basketball goal, and rule #3 must retain basketball player finesse while wearing apparatus.” © 2014.
